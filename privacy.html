Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Quiet Stack Studios, privacy isn't just a feature — it's the foundation of everything we build. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information across all of our applications.

1. Information Collection

We believe in data minimisation. Our apps are designed to function primarily on-device. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect identifiable usage analytics. Where a third-party SDK is used (see §7), this is disclosed explicitly below.

2. On-Device Processing & Screen Time API (Flint)

Flint utilises Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity) to provide its core blocking and reporting functionality. This data is processed entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal browsing history, app usage data, or screen time statistics.

3. Camera, Photos & Notifications (Verdant Garden)

Verdant Garden requests access to your device's Camera and Photo Library solely for the purpose of allowing you to log the visual growth of your plants. These photos remain on your local device or your personal iCloud account. Verdant Garden also uses local notifications to deliver watering reminders, scheduled natively on your device.

4. Mindful Breathing (Oxyn)

Oxyn is designed to guide your breathing practices entirely on-device. We do not record, collect, or monetise your breathing sessions or app usage data. Any preferences or session history remain strictly local on your device.

5. Habit Tracking (Clear Days)

Clear Days stores all habit tracking data — including streaks, relapse history, milestones, and personal motivations — locally on your device. We do not collect, view, or share this information. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account and synced across your devices; we cannot access it. Home screen widgets use on-device App Groups to display your streak data and do not transmit information externally.

6. Photos, Camera & Media (Cubedit)

Cubedit requests access to your device's Photo Library and Camera for the sole purpose of importing photos and videos for editing and exporting finished edits back to your library. We want to be clear about what this means in practice:

  • Your photos and videos are never uploaded to any server. They do not leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them yourself.
  • All image and video processing — including colour grading, LUT application, tone curve adjustments, exposure, contrast, saturation, sharpening, and export rendering — is performed entirely on-device using Apple's CoreImage framework.
  • Cubedit has no backend server, no cloud storage, and no network dependency for its core editing features.
  • Your creative work remains entirely yours. We claim no rights over any content you import into or export from Cubedit.

7. Crash Reporting — Firebase Crashlytics (Cubedit)

Cubedit uses Firebase Crashlytics, a crash reporting service provided by Google LLC, to help us identify and fix bugs. When the app crashes or encounters an error, Crashlytics automatically collects and transmits the following information to Google's servers:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • A randomised Crashlytics installation UUID (not linked to any personal identity)
  • Stack traces and crash logs describing what the app was doing at the time of the error

Crashlytics does not collect your name, email address, Apple ID, photos, videos, or any content you edit within the app. The crash reports are used exclusively for app stability and improvement. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Firebase Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/privacy.

8. iCloud Synchronisation

If you choose to sync your data across devices (where applicable), we utilise Apple's iCloud framework. Your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account. We cannot access, view, or modify it.

9. Third-Party Services Summary

We do not sell your data. We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or tracking pixels into our applications. The only third-party SDK we use is Firebase Crashlytics in Cubedit (§7 above), used solely for crash diagnostics.

10. Australian Privacy Act

Quiet Stack Studios is based in Australia and is mindful of its obligations under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). Because our apps do not collect personal information (name, email, location, or other identifiers) directly, most of the APPs are not triggered. Where Crashlytics transmits limited technical data, it is handled by Google in accordance with their own compliance obligations. If you have any concern about how your information is handled, please contact us at the address below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Where we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our apps after any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@quietstackstudios.com.
