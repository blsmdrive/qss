At Quiet Stack Studios, privacy isn't just a featureâ€”it's the foundation of everything we build. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

We believe in data minimization. Our apps are built to function primarily on-device. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect identifiable usage analytics without your explicit, opt-in consent.

2. On-Device Processing & Screen Time API (Flint)

Flint utilize Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity) to provide its core blocking and reporting functionality. This data is processed entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal browsing history, app usage data, or screen time statistics.

3. Camera, Photos & Notifications (Verdant Garden)

Verdant Garden requests access to your device's Camera and Photo Library solely for the purpose of allowing you to log the visual growth of your plants. These photos remain on your local device or your personal iCloud account. Verdant Garden also utilizes local notifications to deliver watering reminders, scheduled natively on your device.

4. iCloud Synchronization

If you choose to sync your data across devices, we utilize Apple's iCloud framework. Your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account. We cannot access, view, or modify it.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or tracking pixels into our applications.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@quietstackstudios.com.
