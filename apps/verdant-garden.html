Verdant Garden is a free iOS app that helps you identify plants using your camera and manage your plant collection with a watering calendar, personalised care tips, and a database of 77,000+ species.

Verdant uses the Pl@ntNet API, a leading botanical recognition engine. Simply take a photo of a leaf, flower, or whole plant, and the app returns identification results with confidence scores. Results include confidence levels to help you assess accuracy, but we recommend cross-referencing for critical decisions.
