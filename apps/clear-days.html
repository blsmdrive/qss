Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Days is a free iOS app designed to help you break bad habits. Track how long you've been clean from any habit — from smoking and social media to procrastination and impulse spending. With streak tracking, milestone celebrations, and a supportive relapse flow, it's your personal companion on the journey to a better you.

When you add a habit, you set your quit date and time. Clear Days then counts every day, hour, minute, and second since you quit. You'll see your current streak, your best streak, total relapses, and your start date — all at a glance from the habit detail screen.

Slip-ups are a natural part of breaking habits. Clear Days lets you log a relapse without judgment — your streak resets, but your overall progress and history are preserved. The app encourages you to get back on track with supportive messaging because every fresh start counts.
