Frequently Asked Questions

Consilient is a free iOS note-taking app built around a spatial canvas. Instead of a linear list, your folders live on a 2D canvas you can zoom, pan, and arrange freely. Tag your folders and Consilient automatically reveals visual connections between related topics — turning your notes into a knowledge map. Add attachments, search across everything, and sync it all in real time via Apple CloudKit.

Your canvas is a 2D workspace where folders appear as draggable objects. You can zoom from 0.3x to 3.0x, pan in any direction, and tag your folders to reveal visual connections between related topics. It's like a whiteboard that maps the relationships between your ideas — the more you tag, the more your knowledge graph grows.

Yes. Consilient uses Apple CloudKit for real-time synchronisation. Your notes, folders, tags, canvas layout, and connections sync automatically across your iPhone devices signed into the same iCloud account.

You can attach photos from your library, capture new photos with your camera, and add documents directly to any note. All attachments sync via CloudKit alongside your note content.

Consilient is available for iPhone running iOS 17 or later. Your data syncs via iCloud so you always have access to your notes.

Yes. Consilient syncs exclusively through Apple CloudKit — your data lives in your personal iCloud account. There are no third-party servers, no analytics tracking, and no account sign-up required.
