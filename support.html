Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a subscription?
All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions to manage or cancel.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us directly using the contact information provided on this page. Include your device model and iOS version if possible.
Do you collect my data?
No. We believe in privacy by design. Our apps use on-device processing and iCloud sync where applicable. We don't have access to your personal data.
